Features

Adorable Ducks

Traditional black and white pieces are replaced by cute little ducks, making the game more lively and fun.

Classic Gomoku Rules

Line up five ducks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win the game.

Turn-Based Gameplay

Two players take turns placing their ducks. Each turn only allows one duck placement on the board.

Draw Possibility

If the board is full and neither player has five in a row, the game ends in a draw.

How to Play

Step 1

Take turns placing your own little duck on an empty intersection on the board.

Step 2

Once placed, a duck cannot be moved or removed.

Step 3

The first player to line up five ducks in a straight line wins!

Step 4

If the board is filled and no player has five in a row, the game ends in a draw.
